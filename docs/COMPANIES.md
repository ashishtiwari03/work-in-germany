## 📝 How to Research a Company

Before applying, check these sources:

1. **[Kununu](https://www.kununu.com)** — Germany's Glassdoor equivalent. Most DE companies have reviews here.
2. **[Glassdoor](https://www.glassdoor.de)** — International reviews and salary data.
3. **[Levels.fyi](https://www.levels.fyi/?country=Germany)** — Tech compensation data.
4. **LinkedIn** — Search current employees, check team diversity, see if internationals work there.
5. **Company tech blog** — Signals engineering culture and stack.
6. **GitHub org** — Open source contributions indicate engineering maturity.
