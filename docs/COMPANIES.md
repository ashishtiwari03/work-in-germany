## 📝 How to Research a Company

Before applying, check these sources:

1. **[Kununu](https://www.kununu.com)** — The German Glassdoor. Most companies have reviews here.
2. **[Glassdoor](https://www.glassdoor.de)** — International reviews, salary data.
3. **[Levels.fyi](https://www.levels.fyi/?country=Germany)** — Tech compensation data.
4. **LinkedIn** — Search for employees, see team diversity, check if current internationals work there.
5. **Company tech blog** — Shows engineering culture and stack.
6. **GitHub organization** — Open source contributions indicate engineering maturity.
